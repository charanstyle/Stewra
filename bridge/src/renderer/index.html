Experimental. This links your personal WhatsApp account using an unofficial method that WhatsApp does not permit. Your account can be permanently banned, and bans are usually not reversible. Only link an account you are willing to lose.

On your phone, open

WhatsApp → Linked Devices → Link a device

then point your phone at this code. It refreshes every few minutes.

You will see Stewra Bridge in WhatsApp → Linked Devices. You can remove it from your phone at any time, and it will stop immediately.

Stewra only answers while this app is running. WhatsApp also unlinks companion devices if your phone stays offline for about 14 days.
